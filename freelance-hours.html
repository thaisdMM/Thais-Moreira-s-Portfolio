Freelance Hours

Homepage

On the homepage, we have the Freelance Hours logo, a link to post a project, another to search for a project, and another explaining how the project works. There is also a photo. On the homepage, we also display whether a project is closed or open, the project name, the date it was published, and the closing time. Additionally, it includes the technologies used and the name of the person who posted the project.

Accepting Proposals

On the “Accepting Proposals” page, the title indicates that proposals are open, followed by the project name, a short description, the publication date, a link to submit a proposal, and a countdown showing the remaining time until the deadline. The page also lists the technologies used in the project and the name of the person who posted it. On the right side, there is a ranking of submitted proposals, displaying the applicants emails, the time since submission, and the estimated hours to complete the project. If needed, there is an option to load more proposals.
